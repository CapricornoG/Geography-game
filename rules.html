Geography Game Rules

Welcome to the Geography Game! Here are the rules:

  1. Guess the correct answer for each question.
  2. You have 30 seconds to answer each question.
  3. Each correct answer earns you 1 point.
  4. Answers will be color-coded: Green for correct and Red for incorrect.
  5. The game finishes after answering all the questions.

Test your geography knowledge and see how many points you can score!
